# Claude Review Pack

This document exists because the live site can return `403` to non-browser crawlers behind Cloudflare.

Use this pack as the canonical review input for Claude.

## Primary goal

Audit the public marketing and conversion site for the RPMS training offer from `CITYZ'France`.

Focus on:

- homepage clarity
- program page credibility
- financing page conversion
- CTA hierarchy
- visual consistency
- trust signals
- friction in the signup path

## What the site sells

The site sells one main offer:

- `Titre professionnel Responsable de petite et moyenne structure (RPMS)`

Core positioning:

- Bac+2 / niveau 5
- accessible without a long traditional academic path
- 100% remote
- 364 hours
- includes pedagogical support

## Current financing message

The current financing logic exposed to users is:

- one-time payment
- internal installment plan with no fees
- state or employer-backed funding

Important:

- do not mention Alma in the review, the site is moving away from that wording

## Main pages to assess

### Home

Intent:

- create urgency
- establish legitimacy
- explain who the training is for
- push toward diagnostic or signup

Things to inspect:

- hero readability
- density of sections
- sequence of persuasion
- testimonial credibility
- financing block integration

### Programme

Intent:

- make the training feel substantial, official, and outcome-oriented

Things to inspect:

- hero balance between authority and simplicity
- “Pourquoi le RPMS ?”
- “Transformation garantie”
- program blocks readability
- objective list clarity
- usefulness of brochure link

### Financement

Intent:

- reduce payment anxiety
- make the installment plan feel simple
- support conversion to signup

Things to inspect:

- whether the three options are instantly understandable
- whether the featured card is the right one
- whether the labels and CTA wording reduce friction

## Expected output from Claude

Please return:

1. a score out of 20
2. the top 5 issues, ordered by severity
3. the top 5 strengths
4. concrete rewrite suggestions for weak headings or CTAs
5. any design inconsistencies that reduce trust or conversion

## Constraints for the review

- review the site as a French conversion site, not as a SaaS app
- optimize for credibility and enrollment
- avoid generic design advice
- be specific about which page and which block is weak or strong
- if something feels repetitive, say exactly what should be removed or merged
